Railway Reservation & Ticketing. It'll blow your mind.

Railway transport considers the processes of ticketing and reservation to be one and the same since there’s no delay or distinction between booking and getting a train ticket, unlike in air transport. Ticketing functionality covers customer-facing features that make it convenient for customers to choose, pay for, use, and print tickets. Some of the most crucial ticketing implementations in a modern reservation platform include the following.

Service special request.See for yourself.

Normalized by airlines, SSRs or special service requests are provided at the time of booking by travelers with disabilities and special needs to let the provider know how they can prepare. In the railway industry, such requests are usually made by phone or by speaking with someone at the station. But there are better ways to implement this, for example, by including an online form within the booking flow. Other features that can enhance the booking experience include: loyalty program design, multi-language and multi-currency support, seat maps, email, SMS, or in-app notifications, multiple payment options integration, easy rebooking and cancellation capabilities, automated refunds.
